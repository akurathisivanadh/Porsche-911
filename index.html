Carrera: Timeless Design

Entry-level rear-wheel drive 911 featuring classic sports car proportions, lightweight construction, and everyday usability. Offers manual or PDK dual-clutch transmission options with rear-engine layout for signature 911 handling characteristics.

Driver-Focused Cockpit

3.0L twin-turbocharged flat-six boxer engine producing 379 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque. Features direct fuel injection, variable valve timing, and integrated dry-sump lubrication for optimal performance and reliability.

Pure Performance

0-60 mph acceleration in 4.0 seconds, top speed of 182 mph, and quarter-mile time of 12.3 seconds. Delivers exceptional fuel economy for a sports car while maintaining Porsche's renowned precision and driving dynamics.

Back

GTS: Sharpened Dynamics

Mid-tier performance variant with sport-tuned suspension, wider rear fenders, and exclusive GTS styling elements. Features center-lock wheels, sport exhaust system, and enhanced interior with Alcantara trim throughout the cabin.

Superior Traction

Enhanced 3.0L twin-turbo flat-six generating 473 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque with improved turbocharger efficiency. Includes sport-tuned intake and exhaust systems for increased power output and distinctive sound character.

More Power, More Thrills

0-60 mph sprint in 3.2 seconds, maximum speed of 193 mph, and quarter-mile completion in 11.3 seconds. Offers the perfect balance of track-focused performance and street comfort with adaptive dampers and multiple drive modes.

Back

Turbo S: The Apex Predator

All-wheel drive flagship featuring active aerodynamics with deployable front spoiler and rear wing, adaptive air suspension, and widebody design. Includes Porsche Traction Management system and advanced stability control for maximum grip and performance.

Active Aerodynamics

High-performance 3.8L twin-turbocharged flat-six producing 572 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque with variable turbine geometry. Features intercooling system, reinforced internals, and optimized turbocharger placement for minimal lag and maximum power delivery.

Unfathomable Power

Blistering 0-60 mph acceleration in 2.7 seconds, top speed of 205 mph, and quarter-mile time of 10.9 seconds. Delivers supercar-level performance with launch control, multiple drive modes, and ceramic composite brakes for consistent stopping power.
